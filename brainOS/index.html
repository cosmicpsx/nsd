Frequently Asked Questions

Is BrainOS installation procedure safe?

BrainOS installation uses our revolutionary NanoLink technology, which requires no surgical procedures. The quantum neural interfaces are administered via a specialized headset during a 3-hour session. The process is completely safe, painless, and has been thoroughly tested to meet the highest medical safety standards.

Can BrainOS be uninstalled if needed?

Yes, BrainOS is fully reversible. Our proprietary Quantum Neural Dissolution protocol can safely remove all traces of the system without affecting your natural neural pathways or memories. The uninstallation process takes approximately 2 hours and can be performed at any of our certified centers.

How does BrainOS protect my privacy?

Privacy is our highest priority. BrainOS operates entirely within your neural network and does not transmit data externally without explicit authorization. Additionally, our Cognitive Firewall technology provides military-grade protection against unauthorized access to your thoughts and neural data.

Will I still feel like myself with BrainOS?

Absolutely. BrainOS is designed to enhance your natural capabilities without altering your personality, memories, or sense of self. The system operates as an extension of your own cognitive processes rather than replacing them. Our extensive testing shows that users consistently report feeling "more themselves" after integration.

How are updates managed?

BrainOS updates are delivered wirelessly during sleep cycles to ensure they don't interfere with daily activities. Each update is thoroughly vetted for compatibility with your specific neural patterns before installation. You maintain full control over which updates to accept and when to install them.
